About Our Project

This is a responsive website with a feed button that connects to a system at home to feed your dogs, anytime and anywhere.
This website also proposes a prototype system associated with the project mentioned.

WHY?

Our pet dogs deserve all the love even if we are away for a few hours. We want to promote animal care and provide a free sevice of dispensing food in your pet's bowls when you are away. With a click of a button and wifi connection, you can give all your love!


HOW?

With the use of hard ware components like NODE MCU, servo motor SG90 we are able to build a prototype of this project.

WHAT?

This is responsive website powering a home based system, that automaticallu dispenses food.
This system is for those who are frequently and temporarily away from home but want to ensure their dog is fed properly.
